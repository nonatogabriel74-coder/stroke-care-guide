# Engenharia de Prompts

## Prompt 1

Summarize the main causes, symptoms, prevention methods, and treatment options for stroke.

Objetivo:
Obter uma visão geral do AVC.

---

## Prompt 2

Compare ischemic stroke and hemorrhagic stroke regarding causes, symptoms, diagnosis, treatment, and prognosis.

Objetivo:
Comparar os dois principais tipos de AVC.

---

## Prompt 3

Create a rehabilitation roadmap for stroke survivors covering physical, cognitive, emotional, and social recovery.

Objetivo:
Desenvolver um plano de reabilitação.

---

## Prompt 4

Identify the most effective evidence-based prevention strategies for reducing stroke risk.

Objetivo:
Identificar medidas preventivas eficazes.
